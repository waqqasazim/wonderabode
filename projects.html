Our Projects

Lansdowne

Lansdowne is a quaint hill station nestled in the Pauri Garhwal district of Uttarakhand, India. Established in 1887 as a British cantonment, it remains largely unspoiled and offers a tranquil escape amidst the Himalayas.

Sonapani estate

Nestled in the Nainital district of Uttarakhand, Sonapani village is a haven for nature lovers. Surrounded by pine, oak, and rhododendron forests, it offers stunning 175-degree views of the Himalayas. The village sits at an elevation of 2000 meters and boasts orchards bursting with plums, apples, peaches, and apricots.

New Tehri

New Tehri is a modern town nestled in the Tehri Garhwal district of Uttarakhand, India. This planned town serves as the administrative headquarters for the district. New Tehri boasts stunning views of the gigantic Tehri Lake, created by one of India’s largest hydroelectric dams.
